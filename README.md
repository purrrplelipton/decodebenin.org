# Decode Benin - Beyond the Degree

Decode Benin is a knowledge-driven community and digital infrastructure designed to bridge the gap between academic education and the practical tech ecosystem in Benin Republic. We empower students and youth with immersive skill learning, industry mentorship, and high-impact career opportunities.
